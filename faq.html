Minimum Order Quantities

What is the minimum bulk order quantity?

Minimum order quantity depends on the wood type and delivery location, so we confirm it during the enquiry stage.

Can I mix more than one wood type in the same order?

Yes, mixed requirements can usually be arranged. Share the wood types and quantity split when you enquire.

Do you handle recurring orders for factories and hotels?

Yes, repeat buyers are a core part of the business and we plan supply around recurring requirements.

Delivery Times

How soon can an order be dispatched?

Dispatch timing depends on quantity, product availability, and delivery location. We confirm the timeline after checking the requirement.

Do you support planned delivery slots?

Yes, we can coordinate delivery timing so the load reaches at the expected time as closely as possible.

Do you provide transport support?

Yes, transport planning is part of the service so larger orders stay organized from preparation to delivery.

Payment Terms

What payment modes do you accept?

Payment modes are confirmed during the enquiry so the buyer gets a clear next step before dispatch.

Do you require advance confirmation?

For bulk orders, we generally confirm the requirement and payment arrangement before moving ahead.

Can you share quotation details first?

Yes, you can contact us directly to discuss quantity, wood type, and delivery needs before finalizing the order.
